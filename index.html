Smart Matching

Browse partners like Netflix. Curated categories like "Picked for You," "People Local to You," and "Academic Focus" make finding the right match effortless.

Swipe for Translation

Swipe right on any message to instantly see what it means in your language. No copy-pasting into separate apps.

Grammar Help

Swipe left on any message for AI-powered grammar analysis, corrections, alternative expressions, and cultural context.

AI Language Muses

Practice 24/7 with AI-powered conversation partners. One for every language, always available when humans aren't around.

Language Lab

Track your learning progress with streaks, heat maps, and fluency analytics. See how you're improving over time.

Match Your Way

Strictly platonic mode for learners who want friendship only. Blur your photos until you match. You're in control.
